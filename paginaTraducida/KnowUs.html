Mission

This tool seeks to facilitate the work of researchers in the field of bacterial growth, ideally for purposes focused on bioremediation, although the latter does not limit professionals with an interest in this resource.

Vision

It is expected that within five years, calculating bacteria will become widely used software in research, allowing experts in the area to optimize their time, and eventually implement new mathematical tools that not only model bacterial growth.

Objective 1

Make the quantitative part of a research project more bearable, in order to have more time for the other stages

Objective 2

Provide a comprehensive service that allows monitoring the behavior of unicellular organisms
