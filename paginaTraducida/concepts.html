Bacterial growth

Orderly increase of all the component elements of that system, which implies an increase in cell mass that eventually leads to cell multiplication (colonies).

prokaryotic cell cycle

A cell cycle is a sequence of interconnected events that begins with the formation of a new cell and ends when that cell divides into two more daughter cells.

Measures of the number of individuals

Balanced growth [=Balanced]

A population of bacteria found in a suitable medium in which all their nutritional and environmental parameters are kept constant grows in such a way that the increase per unit time of cell mass, not cells, DNA, RNA, proteins, etc. ., is a constant value and similar in each case
